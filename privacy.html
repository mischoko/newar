GDPR Compliant privacy policy statement

With accordance to Article 13 EU General Data Protection Regulation (EU-GDPR).

As by filling out the contact form consists a provision of your personal information, we would like to inform you about your rights and terms of conditions concerning our privacy policy. We would also like to reassure you that keeping your sensitive information is of utmost importance to us as a company.

The scope of use of your personal information:

All the information that you fill in the contact form such as name, subject, email address, contents of your message and any other data with similar significance are being used and kept solely for the purpose of contacting your and replying to your message.

In case the email was obtained through subscribtion of the user to our monthly newsletter, the act of subscribing supposes that the user has read the privacy policy statement, as stated below in the form fiels, therefore is considered informed and in a full agreement with privacy policy statement.

Applicable rights according to GDPR concerning the storage and handling of personal information:

  • right to ask a permission to access your personal information
  • right to correct or change your personal information
  • right to ask for a removal of your personal information from the database
  • right to modify the range of use of your personal information
  • right to object to the processing of your personal information
  • right to validity of your personal information
  • right to unsubscribe from the newsletter

In case you wish to apply any of the right listed above, please contact us using the email address info@arvisual.eu.

You also possess the right to ask for a discontinuation of storage of your personal information and consequent removal of your personal information from our database. To do so, please contact us using the email address info@arvisual.eu The canceling of your agreement to provide your personal information has no effect whatsoever on previous lawfulness of storage and use of your personal information before the canceling.

The provision of agreement with storing and usage of your personal information is voluntary. Consequence of providing incorrect information is such that we will not be able to contact you back or that your message comes across misconstrued. We do not take any responsibilities for what information you provide us with, although we are bound by law to keep it private.
