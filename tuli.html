Tuli

Original bean bags

Client

Since the year 2006 - Tuli.sk is the largest manufacturer and reseller of original bean bags in Slovakia. Apart from that, they also offer a wide range of seating products, home decor items and toys.

Task

The client has picked the 30 best selling bean bags to be put into their custom AR app. When the customer base gets familiar with the AR appapplication, the client plans on gradually adding new products.

Martin Guttman -
CEO | Tuli

"Custom AR application has really helped with our mission to get closer to our customers. We are able to communicate to the relevant audience which resulted in revenue spike since we have deployed this solution."

Solutions

The main reason behind Tuli getting into the Augmented Reality world was that they wanted to broaden the market and build more engaging relationships with their customers.

Results

After releasing the application, Tuli has noticed an increase in online sales and also an increased engagement around their AR application on social media.
